# psych-kg

A Claude skill backed by a curated knowledge graph of 120 canonical psychology concepts.
Each concept is populated from meta-analyses, systematic reviews, and major handbooks —
consensus-first, with contested frameworks (polyvagal theory, window of tolerance, rigid
attachment type categories) explicitly excluded or marked.

## What's in it

120 concepts across 10 domains:

- Personality structure and individual differences
- Attachment and close relationships
- Cognitive architecture and executive control
- Judgment and decision making
- Learning, reinforcement, and habit
- Stress, trauma, and physiology
- Emotion and emotion regulation
- Mindfulness, metacognition, and repetitive thought
- Motivation and self-regulation
- Change mechanisms, psychotherapy, and social regulation

Each concept includes: dense definition (150-200 words), mechanism, functional role,
measurement methods, developmental notes, clinical relevance, boundary conditions,
common confusions, related/contrast concepts (cross-referenced), and 3-6 citations.

## Usage

Once installed, Claude will use this skill automatically when answering questions about
psychological constructs. You can also trigger it explicitly:

- "What is the mechanism behind cognitive reappraisal?"
- "Compare rumination and worry"
- "What are the boundary conditions for habit formation?"
- "What measurement tools are used for attachment avoidance?"
- "What does the research consensus say about expressive suppression?"
